# The Mist

Generally your best resource for the mist will be the mist document, and video guide created by OxBox linked below.

>[!NOTE]
> The Mist was reworked on patch 1.0.29242, this resulted in some tech like room juggling no longer working.

As a shorthand for how to traverse the mist -  
- In the first two rooms, use Needolin to find the correct exit.
- In the third room, take any left or right exit except where you came from. 
  - On patches 1.0.29242+, if there are more than one of these, use Needolin
- The fourth room is just going straight as an intermission / "Halfway point".
- In the fifth room, use Needolin to find the correct exit.
- In the sixth room, take the rightwards* exit if only one exists, or use the Needolin to find the correct rightwards exit if there are multiple. (*Or leftward if coming from Bilewater.)

On patches below 1.0.29242, at any point you can choose to "juggle" a room, where you will go back a room purposefully, to take a faster exit.

- More info at [Mist Document](<https://docs.google.com/document/d/1wGYxE_mDfZ_Qtbs6cvcTZggTFPYTBJ9Rijno0VZkNdA/edit?usp=sharing>) or [Mist Video Guide](<https://youtu.be/XnCsbhWmSQE>)

- Additionally you may want to visit, the [Mist Strats Thread](https://discord.com/channels/772964112908156938/1526659889512976506)  
A discussion thread for the best Mist strategies (mainly for Any%) within the speedrunning discord.
