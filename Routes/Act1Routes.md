## Judgement Routes
Judgement was a category originally created for an [SRC Sponsored Challenge](https://www.speedrun.com/challenges/vd39zep5-silksong-silksong-judgement-speedrun-challenge) in October 2025 with cash prizes. With the category booming in popularity, as a result of both the challenge, and Act 1 runs being submitted alongside Any% runs, Judgement got it's own category board which was later extended to also include the Sinner category.

- ### Cloakless Flea
Typically ran on 1.0.28714 or lower - To take advantage of a [Bind Dash Refresh](/Movement/GeneralMovement.md#bind-dash-refresh-minor-glitch) in blasted steps. However this can be ran on any patch, if you would like to forgo that specific skip.  

The flea route for Judgement, is quite unique, as it generally follows the same route as [Any%'s flea route](/Routes/Any%25Routes.md#flea-route), with the main exception of **NOT** getting the needle upgrade in Bellhart, immidietly leaving Widow's boss arena from the top left, instead of right, and grabbing bells along the way.   

>[Flea Route Splits](https://hksplitmaker.com/?game=silksong&builtin=act1-judgement-fleas)  
>[Flea Route Savestates]-(DL-LINK) - May be partially outdated, but work as intended  
>No notes, guides, or Livesplit IL's exist, however you can get the general idea of the route by watching runs on [SRC](https://www.speedrun.com/silksong?h=judgement-no-major-glitches-judgement&x=wk6544o2-jlz631q8.1w4ozxvq-j8415y58.qox0j35q)

- ### Cloak Bells
Ran on any Patch. Slower than the above flea route, but more popular.  

The cloak bells route for Judgement is typically just the same as the [Any% Bells route](/Routes/Any%25Routes.md#bell-route), so typically a lot of people will do a Bells route Any% run, and submit it for both Any% and Judgement categories - If you want to be optimal however, Judgement benefits from **NOT** getting the nail upgrade in Bellhart, so the savestates below do not include getting that upgrade.

>[Bell Route Splits](https://hksplitmaker.com/?game=silksong&builtin=act1-judgement)  
>[Bell Route Savestates]-(DL-LINK) - These are simply [Any% Bells](/Routes/Any%25Routes.md#bell-route) savestates that have been altered to have no nail upgrades, and removed all states irrelevent to Judgement  
>No notes, guides, or Livesplit IL's exist, however you can get the general idea of the run by watching runs on [SRC](https://www.speedrun.com/silksong?h=judgement-no-major-glitches-judgement&x=wk6544o2-jlz631q8.1w4ozxvq-j8415y58.qox0j35q)

## Sinner Routes
The Sinner category was created as a follow up to Judgement's category creation. Since both Act 1 Bells and Mist routes were used in Any%, as more and more Bell, and Key route runs were completed, it was heavily requested a board for starting Act 2 through the Mist (and Phantom) was added.

- ### Cloakless Key
A lot of Sinner runs are submitted on Release Patch (1.0.28324) however that is simply an effect of people submitting runs from their [Any% Key route](/Routes/Any%25Routes.md#key-route) runs, which in itself is ran on RP.

Typically running on 1.0.28891 or earlier is recommended in order to still have access to [Mist](/GameMechanics/TheMist.md) room juggling.

Technically you *could* run on 1.0.29315 or later in order to take advantage of better rosary routing (that saves roughly 13 seconds over RP rosary routing) with thanks from the added rosaries in that patch, such as the string of 30 rosaries at the elevator near Swift Step. However doing so would mean having to deal with the [Mist](/GameMechanics/TheMist.md) rework, which means no room juggling, and a heavily RNG [Mist](/GameMechanics/TheMist.md) section. Because of this, very few people have ran on later patches, in favour of an easier and less RNG [Mist](/GameMechanics/TheMist.md).

Additionally if running the category optimally, you should **NOT** get the needle upgrade when reaching Bellhart. As such the savestates below have removed the needle upgrade, but otherwise are very similar to [Any% Key route's](/Routes/Any%25Routes.md#key-route) Savestates.

>[Key Route Splits](https://hksplitmaker.com/?game=silksong&builtin=act1-sinner)  
>[Key route savestates]-(DL-LINK) - These savestates have been taken from [Any% Key Route](/Routes/Any%25Routes.md#key-route) and altered to have no nail upgrade, and removed savestates irrelevant to Sinner.  
>No notes, guides, or Livesplit IL's exist, however you can get the general idea of the route by watching runs on [SRC](https://www.speedrun.com/silksong?h=judgement-no-major-glitches-sinner&x=wk6544o2-jlz631q8.1w4ozxvq-j8415y58.1390xmr1)

- ### Cloakless Flea
Ran on the same patches as the above key route, as such the same general information applies, but ultimately Flea route is much slower than key.

Additionally if running the category optimally, you should **NOT** get the needle upgrade when reaching Bellhart.

No Resources are provided for this route, however the [Any% Flea Route](/Routes/Any%25Routes.md#flea-route) resources can serve a similar function, should you want them.
